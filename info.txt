1. Uruchomić sshd
2. Utworzyc konto recovery
3. Zakomentowac w /etc/pam.d/sshd
...
# Print the message of the day upon successful login.
# This includes a dynamically generated part from /run/motd.dynamic
# and a static (admin-editable) part from /etc/motd.
session    optional     pam_motd.so  motd=/run/motd.dynamic
session    optional     pam_motd.so noupdate
...
4. Zmienic w /etc/passwd
...
recovery:x:0:0::/home/recovery:/home/recovery/qnx_backup_and_recovery/recovery.sh
...
5. Dodac zmienna srodowiskowa QNX_BACKUP_AND_RECOVERY_PATH=/home/recovery/qnx_backup_and_recovery (cp .profile ~/).
6. Ustawic strefe czasowa:
timedatectl
timedatectl list-timezones
timedatectl set-timezone Europe/Warsaw
7. Wygenerowac klucze SSH (ssh-keygen) i skopiowac klucz publiczny na stacje QNX (ssh_copy_id_to_hosts.sh).
8. Na komputerach z systemem QNX zainstalować rsync (ssh_copy_rsync.sh).
9. Skopiowanie qnx_backup_and_recovery.sh do /etc/profile.d
